Why use it

Streamline incident handling across your organization

Record incident metadata (type, severity, location, attachments), assign responsible staff, track status and deadlines, and produce reports for analysis and compliance.

Notifications keep responsible personnel informed and built-in search & filters help monitor incident history and spot patterns.
